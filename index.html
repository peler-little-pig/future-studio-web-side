工作室简介

Introduction

未来工作室是一个独立游戏开发工作室，由几名怀揣梦想的学生创立，首创于2022年7月28日，由于内部原因，直到2023年六月20日才开始第一次开工制作游戏。我们会不忘初心，在游戏开发的道路上愈走愈远！

Future Studio is a independent game development studio. Set up by several students with dreams. First created on July 28, 2022, For internal reasons, it wasn't until June 20, 2023 that the first start of production of the game began. We will not forget our original intention, and go further and further on the road of game development!
